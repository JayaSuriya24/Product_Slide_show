Low Alloy Electrodes For Low Temperatures

Conformance

AWS : E7018       IS : EB5426H3JX

Characteristics

→A heavy coated low hydrogen, iron powder type electrode ideally suited for producing tough and ductile welds of radiographic quality in boilers, pressure vessels and heavy structures subjected to dynamic loading. The electrodes have a metal recovery of about 115%.

Key features

→Basic type iron powder electrode
→All position capability
→Radiographic weld deposit
→Suitable for pipe welding in 5G & 6G positions.

Typical Application

→Pressure vessels, Pipes
→Storage tanks
→Bridges, Heavy structures
→Joining steel of ASTM SA 414/414M Gr.C/D, SA 516/516M Gr.55/60, IS 2002, IS 2062.

Precaution

→Ensure the electrodes are dry. Re-dry the moist electrodes as per the standard recommended practice

Low Alloy Electrodes For Low Temperatures

Conformance

AWS : E7018-1       IS : EB5626H3JX

Characteristics

→An iron powder, low hydrogen type electrode producing a tough and ductile weld metal for welding heavy and rigid structures subjected to dynamic loading and impact.

Key features

→Basic coated iron powder type
→Suitable for pipe welding in 5G, 6G & 6GR positions
→Excellent toughness down to -45ºC
→Radiographic weld deposit
→All position capability.

Typical Application

→Storage tanks, pipes, boilers
→Bridges & heavy structures subject to dynamic loading
→Joining steel of ASTM SA 414/414M Gr.C/D, SA 516/516M Gr.55/60, IS 2002, IS 2062.

Precaution

→Re-dry the electrodes at 250-300°C for one hour and cool them to 100°C transfer them to a holding oven at 50-60°C and use.
→Minimize heat input.

Cutting & Gouging Electrodes

Electrode for cutting without Oxygen

Procedure

→After striking the arc, swing the arc back and forth as in sawing. Maintain the motion and at the same time dig the arc deeper and deeper in to the metal. For piercing holes, push the arc in and out until the metal is pierced. Use DC with electrode negative for best results.

Key Features

→Cutting electrode with special coating
→ The kerfs are clean and narrow
→Produce negligible slag
→Suitable for all positions
→Special coating gives a stable arc during the cutting or piercing process.

Typical Application

→Cutting and piercing of steel, cast iron, copper materials, aluminium
→ Cutting out unwanted metal in foundry castings
→Excellent for burning rivets
→Dismantling work at sites
→Oxyacetylene or compressed air need not be used.

Hardfacing Electrodes

Characteristics

Basic coated AC / DC(+) electrode producing an air hardening weld metal which has excellent resistance to abrasion or heavy impact or both together. The welds are non-machinable and are ideally suited for applications involving severe abrasion and impact. Typical applications include cane cutting knives, crusher hammers, jaws, rollers, rock drills, tractor grousers, etc.

Key Features

→Machinable with carbide tools
→Resistant to friction
→Good combination of abrasion and toughness
→Recommended buffer layer of Tenalloy-16 on hard base materials.

Typical Application

→ Conveyor parts
→ Brake shoes, Gear shafts
→Excavators, Plough shares
→ Cold punching dies
→ Shear blades .

Creep Resistant Electrodes

Conformance

AWS : E8018-B2       IS : E55 B B2 2 6 Fe

Characteristics

→A low hydrogen iron powder type electrode yielding a weld deposit containing 1.25% Cr - 0.5% Mo. Excellent for welding creep resistant 0.5Cr - 0.5Mo, 1Cr - 0.5Mo steels. The weld deposit has excellent creep resistance at service temperatures up to 550°C..

Key features

→Preheat and interpass temperature of 150-200°C followed by PWHT
→Radiographic quality weld deposit
→Positional welding capability.

Typical Application

→ Welding of 1.25Cr-0.5Mo, 1Cr-0.5Mo steels in refineries, power plants, chemical plants
→Pressure vessels and Boilers br → Cr and Cr-Mo bearing steels at elevated temperature service e.g. steam production plants, steam pipes →Joining P4 materials e.g. ASTM SA 182/182M Gr.F2/F11/F12, SA 213/213M Gr.T11/T12, SA 335/335M Gr.P11/P12, SA 387/387M Gr.2/11/12 → Suitable for 13CrMo44, 15CrMo5, 15Cr3, 16MnCr5, 20MnCr5.

Precaution

→Redry the electrodes as per our standard recommended practice .
→ Use short arc during welding.
